About Us

The Harris County Sheriff's Office is a state-certified law-enforcement agency within the FiveM role-play universe. Our deputies patrol the county around the clock, providing rapid response, criminal investigations, traffic enforcement, and community-oriented policing. We are committed to transparency, accountability, and the highest standards of service.

Contact / Recruitment

Interested in joining our team? Reach out in-city via the MDT dispatch system, or speak with any on-duty supervisor. We are always looking for motivated, professional players to wear the badge.
